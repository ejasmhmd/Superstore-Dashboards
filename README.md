
# Superstore Sales & Profitability Dashboard w/Power BI
 ![Dashboard Page 1](/Images/Superstore_Page%201.png)

 ## Introduction

This project presents a two-page interactive Power BI dashboard built using the Superstore retail dataset. The objective was to analyze revenue performance, identify profitability drivers, detect loss-making segments, and evaluate the impact of discounting on margins.

## Objectives

- Monitor overall sales and profit performance

- Analyze category and sub-category profitability

- Identify regional underperformance

- Quantify total loss from unprofitable products

- Evaluate the relationship between discount levels and profit


## Dashboard Structure
### Page 1 – Executive Overview

![Dashboard Page 1](/Images/Superstore_Page%201.png)

Purpose: High-level performance summary

Includes:

- KPI Cards: Total Sales, Total Profit, Profit Margin, Total Orders

- Monthly Sales vs Profit Trend

- Sales by Category

- Total Profit by Sub-Category

Key Focus:

- Revenue growth vs margin consistency

- Identification of unprofitable sub-categories

### Page 2 – Profitability Deep Dive

![Dashboard Page 1](/Images/Superstore_Page%202.png)

Purpose: Root-cause investigation

Includes:

- Profit by Region

- Discount vs Profit (Scatter Analysis)

- Bottom 15 Loss-Making Products

- Total Loss KPI

- Interactive slicers (Year, Category, Region)

Key Focus:

- Regional imbalance

- Concentration of losses

- Impact of discounting on profitability


## Key Insights

- Revenue growth does not consistently translate into profit growth.

- Losses are concentrated in a small subset of products.

- Certain sub-categories consistently generate negative profit.

- Higher discount levels are associated with reduced profitability in several segments.

- Regional performance varies significantly, impacting overall margin stability.

## Skills Demonstrated

- DAX measure development

- KPI design and financial metric calculation

- Interactive dashboard structuring

- Pricing impact analysis

- Business storytelling with visuals

- Root-cause profitability investigation

## Business Impact Perspective

The dashboard highlights how discount-heavy and structurally unprofitable products significantly erode overall profitability, emphasizing the importance of pricing discipline and portfolio optimization.
